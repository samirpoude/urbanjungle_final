Plant Care Guide

Everything you need to know to keep your indoor plants healthy and thriving. From beginner basics to troubleshooting common issues.

Care Tips by Plant Type

Beginner-Friendly Plants

Includes: Snake Plant, Pothos, ZZ Plant, Spider Plant

  • Start with plants that tolerate neglect and low light conditions
  • Water only when the top inch of soil feels dry
  • Place in bright, indirect light for best results
  • Don't worry about occasional missed waterings
  • Watch for yellowing leaves as a sign of overwatering

Medium Care Plants

Includes: Monstera Deliciosa, Fiddle Leaf Fig, Peace Lily

  • Maintain consistent watering schedules
  • Provide bright, indirect light (avoid direct sun)
  • Monitor humidity levels - use a humidifier if needed
  • Rotate plants weekly for even growth
  • Wipe leaves clean monthly to remove dust

General Plant Care

Includes: All Indoor Plants

  • Check soil moisture before watering - stick your finger 1-2 inches deep
  • Ensure pots have drainage holes to prevent root rot
  • Use room temperature water when watering
  • Fertilize monthly during spring and summer growing season
  • Repot when roots become crowded (usually every 1-2 years)

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Yellow Leaves

Common Causes:

  • Overwatering
  • Natural aging
  • Nutrient deficiency

Solutions:

  • Reduce watering frequency
  • Remove yellow leaves
  • Consider fertilizing

Brown Leaf Tips

Common Causes:

  • Low humidity
  • Fluoride in water
  • Underwatering

Solutions:

  • Increase humidity
  • Use filtered water
  • Check soil moisture more frequently

Drooping Leaves

Common Causes:

  • Underwatering
  • Overwatering
  • Temperature stress

Solutions:

  • Check soil moisture
  • Ensure proper drainage
  • Move away from heat/cold sources

Slow Growth

Common Causes:

  • Insufficient light
  • Lack of nutrients
  • Root bound

Solutions:

  • Move to brighter location
  • Fertilize during growing season
  • Consider repotting
